[DELIVERABLES]
L’Oréal Paris Annual Report

[COURSE NAME]
GR 601 Type Systems

[PROJECT NAME]
Into the World of L’Oréal Paris

[INSTRUCTOR]
John Nettleton

[SEMESTER]
Spring 2021

 

[OBJECTIVE]
Choose a company in a field you are interested in, and select an annual report or a CSR report online that you can harvest text from to redesign. Their annual report should include the director’s/CEO’s letter/introduction charts, graphs, and tables of information. The goal was to identify the visual language of the company/organization and design a multi-page report.

[APPROACH]
L’Oréal is the archetype of the “Beauty Pure Player”—nothing but Beauty, for all types of Beauty—and their unique expertise acquired over 110 years, allows them to understand, in extreme detail, the desires and aspirations of consumers all around the world. However, the annual report lacked consistency and simplicity. I started with developing its visual language and aimed to make it look more sophisticated and approachable. I used the diversity in images to emphasize L’Oréal’s aim of “Beauty for All”. I also paid attention to the layout and composition using hierarchy, scale, and contrast with a limited color palette.

[KEYWORDS]
Diversity, Sophisticated, Subtle

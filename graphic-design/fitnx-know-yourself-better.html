[COURSE NAME]
GR 620 Visual Thinking

[PROJECT NAME]
Fitnx—Know Yourself Better

[INSTRUCTOR]
Sandra Isla

[SEMESTER]
Spring 2021

 

[OBJECTIVE]
In this project, I had to find a fact that might be a problem in the world, one that I would like to see changed. Make a brand that can support or solve the problem. And select three deliverables (product, service, narrative, environment, event, media channel, or digital media) that will best represent my brand.

[APPROACH]
This Project uses a graphic design approach to solve a real-world problem and represents a detailed research process. I started by discovering problems, identifying audience and stakeholders, analyzing opportunities and threats, finding the best solutions, and designing a suitable and engaging visual system. The brand gives an energetic and positive feeling to the audience. I have used colors that represent fun, enthusiasm, and power to attract users’ attention. The brand could help people live an active and energetic lifestyle. Also, using Myriad Pro sans serif typeface with graphic elements evokes the feeling of friendliness and bold weight for power.

[KEYWORDS]
Motivation, Friendly, Active

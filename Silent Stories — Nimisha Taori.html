Silent Stories

Project Overview

  • Gender-based violence is a persistent global issue causing widespread harm to women, leaving them vulnerable. The absence of a unified platform for change leveraging art and artists' voices is a critical gap. Committed artists often go unnoticed, emphasizing the urgent need for a dedicated platform to amplify their voices against gender-based violence.

  • The aim is to create a unified and powerful platform that amplifies the voices of artists and mobilizes individuals, organizations, and communities to take action against gender-based violence. Through art, awareness, resources, and collaboration, we can make significant strides toward ending violence against women and promoting women's safety globally.

The primary goal is to promote artists who are dedicated to advocating for women's safety and ending gender-based violence through their work.

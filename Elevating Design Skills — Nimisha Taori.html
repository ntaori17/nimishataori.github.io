Elevating Design Skills

Project Overview

  • Create a cohesive visual identity for a design conference, encompassing a promotional poster, direct mail piece, website, program, Zoom background, and mobile app. The design must convey a strong conceptual and typographic system, aligning colors, typography, and visuals, while effectively presenting conference information and engaging attendees.

  • Clarity, a design systems conference, emphasizes collaborative work. The design concept of "Collaboration and Conversation" is executed through overlapping elements and scale, symbolizing the convergence of design, development, and product viewpoints. This design underscores skill elevation via inclusive, empathetic, tech-driven, and creative collaboration. This design approach aims to captivate and engage conference attendees while maintaining a coherent visual identity across all materials.

GOALS

The primary goal is to emphasize and promote collaborative work among attendees. By focusing on the "Collaboration and Conversation" design concept, the conference aims to foster interaction and cooperation among participants from diverse design, development, and product backgrounds.

Promote Collaborative Work

Another key goal is skill elevation. The conference intends to provide attendees with opportunities to enhance their skills through various means, including workshops, discussions, and presentations. The emphasis on empathy, technology, creativity, and collaboration suggests a focus on skill development.

Enhance Skills

Maintain Consistent Visual Identity

Ensuring a consistent visual identity across all conference materials is essential. This goal helps create a cohesive and recognizable brand for the conference, making it easier to engage and inform attendees effectively and create a lasting impression.
